About Me

Hi! I am Megh Patel, currently pursuing my bachelors degree in Computer Engineering at Gandhinagar University - Gandhinagar Institute of Technology. I am interested in the field of Computer Science and Engineering, and working towards developing the necessary skills and knowledge to pursue a career in this area.

I am immersing myself in a wide array of subjects, ranging from programming languages to computer systems architecture, and I'm actively engaging in practical learning by participating in lab work, projects, and internships. Beyond my academic pursuits, I am an enthusiastic participant in various extracurricular activities, including clubs and organizations that align with my field of study.

Moreover, I have a genuine curiosity for technology and an unyielding passion for making a meaningful impact on the world through my work. On top of all this, I have an additional facet to my personality—I absolutely love hosting a diverse range of events, which adds an extra layer of excitement to my journey.
